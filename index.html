Summit Edge Media

Summit Edge Media has embraced the broad goal of making the world a better place. We will strive to do this through the software and media that we create and publish to the world. Each app, each piece of software, will allow each and every person around the world to help make a difference in our ever-changing society.

Summit Edge Media is proud to offer a strong plan for how we will grow and expand our product offerings as a company. The first project that we will pursue is SummitVote. SummitVote will be a multi-platform application developed to help make voting easier and more accessible to more people.
